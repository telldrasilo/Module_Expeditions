# 4. Element system

## 4.1 Role of elements

Elements are fundamental forces in the environment, enemies, and resources. They are not abstract “magic” but natural aspects of the world, intensified in certain places.

For the blacksmith, elements matter because:

1. **Weapon scars:** contact with an element leaves hidden damage that, after study and repair, can become an enchantment.
2. **Thematic enchantments:** a weapon’s **War Soul**, awakened after combat experience, grants abilities based on combinations of scars.

## 4.3 Element interactions (optional)

Future enchantment systems may use pairs (e.g. `flame` vs `frost`; `blood` strengthens `bone`). This TZ does not specify that layer in detail.
